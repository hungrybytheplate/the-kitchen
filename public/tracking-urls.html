🔗 The Kitchen - Tracking URLs

Use these UTM-tagged URLs to track which marketing channels drive traffic. View results in Google Analytics → Acquisition → Campaigns.

📊 UTM Parameter Guide

  • utm_source - Where the traffic comes from (instagram, google, email)
  • utm_medium - Marketing medium (social, cpc, email, referral)
  • utm_campaign - Campaign name for grouping
  • utm_content - Specific ad/link variant (optional)
